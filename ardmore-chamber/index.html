What We Heard
A few patterns that
came up in conversation.
  • New members join, attend a ribbon cutting, and then go quiet — because there isn't a structured follow-up process.
  • Members who don't renew probably didn't feel like anything happened for them. We just don't know because there's no exit survey.
  • The current membership packet is dense and wordy. Busy business owners rarely read it closely.
  • The value is real — networking, promotion, events, visibility — but it isn't always easy for members to see it all in one place.
  • Small businesses (the ones most likely to churn) are also the ones least likely to seek out their benefits proactively.

How it works
Enter a URL. Get a shareable story.
We enter a member's website URL. Initiative 04 + 05
Renewals that feel like
a thank you, not an invoice.
Retention Strategy
  • Renewal reminder sequence (60-day, 30-day, 7-day)
    Friendly, value-first touchpoints that build toward renewal — not a single cold invoice.
  • Pre-renewal "here's what's coming" email
    A preview of upcoming events, initiatives, and member benefits for the year ahead.
  • Anniversary celebration posts (1, 5, 10, 20 years)
    Public social recognition that makes long-tenured members feel valued.
  • Exit feedback survey for non-renewing members
    A simple 3–5 question survey sent automatically when a membership lapses.
  • Win-back sequence for lapsed members
    A short re-engagement series highlighting what they've missed and what's coming next.
Annual Visibility Reports
  • Sent to each member once per year, ahead of renewal
    Timed to arrive before the renewal notice so value is top of mind.
  • Documents every Chamber mention, share, and spotlight
    Newsletter features, social posts, event shout-outs — all in one place.
  • Includes estimated audience reach per activity
    So members can see the real exposure their business received.
  • TownSpark page views and CTA clicks
    Direct engagement metrics from their Impact Deck profile.
  • Generated from Jordan's simple activity log — minimal overhead
    A lightweight tracking sheet that feeds into automated report generation.

The Hidden Problem
Your member activity lives in two systems
that don't connect.
The Chamber tracks member relationships in ChamberMaster and sends communications through Mailchimp. These systems don't share data — meaning engagement insights, email opens, event attendance, and renewal signals are scattered across two disconnected platforms. The Visibility Reports and renewal strategy we're proposing would bridge this gap by pulling activity from both systems into a single, member-facing view.
